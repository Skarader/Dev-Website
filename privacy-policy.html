Privacy Policy

App: ColorSpinner

Developer: Skarader

This Privacy Policy explains how ColorSpinner collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information Collection and Use

ColorSpinner does not directly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. However, third-party services integrated into the app may collect certain data as described below.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Audio Settings: To play sound effects and provide audio feedback during gameplay.
  • Advertising ID: Used by Google AdMob to serve personalized advertisements (see Third-Party Services section).

Third-Party Services

ColorSpinner uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. This may include:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • IP address and general location
  • App usage data

For more information about how Google uses data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy Information

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's ad settings or by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Rewarded Video Ads

ColorSpinner offers optional rewarded video advertisements that provide in-game benefits (such as extra lives). Watching these ads is entirely voluntary. When you choose to watch a rewarded ad, the same data collection practices mentioned in the Third-Party Services section apply.

Log Data

ColorSpinner does not directly collect log data. However, third-party services like AdMob may collect crash reports and usage analytics as part of their standard operations.

Cookies

ColorSpinner does not use cookies directly. Third-party advertising services may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver personalized ads.

Children's Privacy

ColorSpinner is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). AdMob ads shown in this app are configured to be family-safe and appropriate for all audiences.

Data Security

We value your trust in using ColorSpinner. While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we rely on commercially acceptable means and trusted third-party services to protect any data processed by the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
